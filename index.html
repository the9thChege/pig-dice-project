THE GAME: Pig Dice

THE RULES:

    1.
  1. The aim of the game is for a player to reach 100 or more points which is calculated cumulatively from the random roll of a 6-sided die
    2. 2.
  2. However, if at any point, the current player rolls a "1", they score nothing for that round and have to forfeit their turn.
  3. Basically, just DON'T roll a "1"!
    4. 3.
  4. You can "Hold" at any point to voluntarily end your turn. Pig Dice is about knowing when to NOT be greedy.

GOOD LUCK!
