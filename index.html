THE GAME: Pig Dice

THE RULES:

    1.
  • The aim of the game is for a player to reach 100 or more points which is calculated cumulatively from the random roll of a 6-sided die
    • 2.
  • However, if at any point, the current player rolls a "1", they score nothing for that round and have to forfeit their turn.
  • Basically, just DON'T roll a "1"!
    • 3.
  • You can "Hold" at any point to voluntarily end your turn. Pig Dice is about knowing when to NOT be greedy.

GOOD LUCK!
